W07 Acorn Project Proposal

Project Ideas for the Four Institutional Learning Outcomes:

• Disciples of Jesus Christ

- Each member comes up with a personal STAR goal that will help them learn to be better disciples of Jesus Christ.

• Sound Thinkers

- Attend a leadership conference/ activity produced by student services

• Effective Communicators

- Meet as a team and present to the team one new thing we learned that week

• Skilled Collaborators

- Work on a project collaboratively using GitHub

Project Influence

• Our project idea for the Disciples of Jesus Christ I.L.O. will positively influence the Belief & Actions portion of the individual team members. This will happen by increasing one’s level of awareness & consciousness.
• For the Sound Thinkers I.L.O. We’ll be positively impacting the community by checking off the representation box.
• Our idea for the Effective Communicators I.L.O. will greatly influence the connectedness and interaction within the team as a group.
• Finally, the skilled collaborators project idea will influence the cooperation, and interaction sections of both community and group areas.

Values

• Disciples of Jesus Christ project: This project reflects our individual and personal standards, personal qualities, and character traits.
• Sound Thinkers project: This project will put into light our ethic codes, principles, and standards of being a member of a community.
• Effective Communicators project: I’m excited to participate in this project. It will reflect the group’s principles, personal qualities, and character traits. This will be a great way to get to know the group better.
• Skilled Collaborators project: This project will reflect upon the group’s standards (when it comes to work), ethic codes, and professional qualities.

Vision

• Our Vision Statement is to Learn and Grow.

SMART Goal

Disciples of Jesus Christ Project:

    • Specific – Each member comes up with one personal goal.
    • Measurable – The team will follow up with every member every week about their progress.
    • Attainable – The team will help determine if an individual team member’s goal is attainable.
    • Relevant – The individual goals must be relevant to the individual.
    • Timely – We will accomplish this goal by the end of the semester.

Sound Thinkers Project:

    • Specific – The entire team must attend one leadership conference or activity.
    • Measurable – This project is measurable through keeping attendance.
    • Attainable – This project is most definitely attainable, because no team member is physically disabled.
    • Relevant – This is relevant, because we must invole ourselves with our communities.
    • Timely – This must be completed by the end of the semester.

Effective Communicators Project:

    • Specific – This project is specific, because we will be discussing one new thing that we learned.
    • Measurable – It will be measurable through attendance.
    • Attainable – This is attainable, because everyone has enough free time to make this happen.
    • Relevant – This is relevant, because it helps us learn more about each other.
    • Timely – This must happen every week.

Skilled Collaborators Project:

    • Specific – This project is specific, because we will be using GitHub.
    • Measurable – We can measure this project by implementing milestones for the project.
    • Attainable – This project is attainable, because all of us are proficient at programming.
    • Relevant – This project is extremely relevant to our group, because we’re all interested in computers & programming.
    • Timely – This project must be finished by the end of the semester.

Audience

• We will be working on these projects within our CSE 170 Technical Teamwork group. We will most likely be presenting them to our peers in that class. However, I’m not sure yet.

Milestones

• Milestones for Disciples of Jesus Christ Project:
    1 – Weekly team follow-ups will ensure that each team member is committed to their personal goal.
    2 – Accomplishment of the personal goal will be by the end of the semester.
    3 – We’ll set aside time to determine the progress of each member’s personal goal.
• Milestones for Sound Thinkers Project:
    1 – Entire team must attend one leadership activity or conference by the end of the semester.
• Milestones for Effective Communicators Project:
    1 – Weekly meetings will be our main milestone for this project.
    2 – This must happen every week until the end of the semester
    3 - We’ll most likely set up a progress bar to keep track of how many weeks we gathered.
• Milestones for Skilled Collaborators Project:
    1 – This project must be completed by the end of the semester.
    2 – We’ll set brief weekly meetings to determine the status of the project.
    3 – We’ll also set milestones for the project according to the requirements that the project has.
